Core Positioning
Differentiation
“Built with evidence, for evidence-based chiropractors.”

ChiroUp EHR stands apart through its redesigned patient and documentation flow:

  • Re-designed patient flow: once the patient enters basic information, the system pre-loads the note, including the subjective.
  • Evidence-based engine: condition flows created from research-backed content, aligned with ChiroUp’s clinical database.
  • Integrated tools: cost integrations and exercise prescriptions built into the workflow.
  • Professional uplift: aims to uplift the profession and provide a platform that integrates chiropractic into the broader healthcare ecosystem.

Funnel
Demo-First Funnel & Conversion Math
Current State
Baseline
~50 demos/month, SPICED-informed.

The current engine delivers roughly 50 demos per month. The methodology follows SPICED (Situation, Pain, Impact, Critical Event, Decision), with demos as the central conversion event.

  • Demos are relationship and education heavy.
  • Evidence and clinical flow are key decision levers.
  • Multiple follow-up touches needed to close.
Target State
Scale
250 demos/month & 40–70 new customers.

At scale, the funnel math looks like:

  • Top of funnel: 250 demos per month.
  • Mid-funnel: ~125 consideration-stage opportunities.
  • Bottom of funnel: 60–70 closed customers (≈25% demo-to-close).

These numbers align with aggressive but achievable SaaS benchmarks given the warm list and clinical credibility.
